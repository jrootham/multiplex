### Financing

Choices
* 5% of capital cost member loan
	* Higher initial outlay
	* Lower rents
	* Lower risk

* 4 months rent
	* Lower initial outlay
	* Higher rents
	* Increased risk

* Community bonds for short term capital
	* Long amortization

* Reserve funds
	* Types
		* General
			* Start low and ramp up
		* Replacement
			* Start low and ramp up
		* Mortgage stabilization
			* Start high
